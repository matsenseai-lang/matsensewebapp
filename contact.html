/ frequently asked

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes — about a third of our work. We have a lighter engagement model (€9–18k, 3–4 weeks) for pre-seed and seed teams shipping v1.

Can you work under NDA?

Of course. Send us one, or we'll send you ours. We're happy to redact any project from our portfolio indefinitely.

Do you offer retainers?

Yes. Most clients stay on a monthly retainer after launch (€4–12k/mo) covering growth, maintenance and a quarterly redesign sprint.

What about international / non-EU clients?

Half our work is outside the EU. Invoicing in EUR or USD, English as default, async by design.
